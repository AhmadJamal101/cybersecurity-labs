## Room

## Objective
Learn the basic concept of computer networking and understand how devices communicate with each other.

## Key Concepts Learned

- What a network is
- How devices communicate

## My Understanding

A network is a group of connected devices that can communicate and share data with each other.

In cybersecurity, networking is important because most attacks, defenses, monitoring, and investigations happen through network communication.
